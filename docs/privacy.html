Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2025

At AuthenticatorPlus ("the Extension"), we prioritize your privacy and security. Our extension is designed to operate entirely on your device, and we collect no data from our users.

No Data Collection

We want to be absolutely clear:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your usage of the extension
  • We do not store any of your authentication data on our servers
  • We do not have access to any of your authentication secrets or accounts

Your Data Storage

All your authentication data is:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Optionally synced through your Google Chrome sync storage (if enabled)
  • Encrypted and secure on your device
  • Completely under your control

Camera Permission

The Extension requests camera access solely for scanning QR codes during account setup. This permission:

  • Is only used when you explicitly choose to scan a QR code
  • Can be revoked at any time through your browser settings
  • Is not used to collect or transmit any images or data

Chrome Sync

If you choose to use Chrome's sync feature:

  • Your encrypted authentication data is synced across your devices through Google's sync infrastructure
  • This sync is handled entirely by Google Chrome's built-in sync functionality
  • You can disable sync at any time through Chrome's settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the Extension and updating the "Last Updated" date.
